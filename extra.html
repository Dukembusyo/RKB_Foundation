Research & Development

We conduct impactful studies that inform public discourse, influence policy, and drive action. Our research spans the following areas:

Disability Inclusion

In-depth analysis of systemic barriers and proposed solutions for inclusive development.

Education Access

Researching education trends, inclusive curriculum, and equitable learning environments.

Economic Empowerment

Findings on vocational training, employment equity, and accessible entrepreneurship models.

Healthcare & Policy

Investigating healthcare accessibility and advocating for disability-sensitive policy reforms.
