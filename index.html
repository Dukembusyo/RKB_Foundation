Who We Are

RKB Foundation is a non profit organization empowering Persons with Disabilities through Capacity building, Education, Policy Advocacy and Research Consultancy.

Vision Statement:

A society where persons with disabilities are empowered, skilled, and actively shaping an innovative and optimistic future. The Future is Bright

Values:

  • Accountability
  • Leadership
  • Teamwork
  • Inclusivity

Our Mission

To empower Persons with disabilities through capacity building, policy advocacy, education access and research development in order to create an innovative and optimistic community. Our core Objectives:

  • 1. Educational Access:
  • 2. Policy Advocacy.
  • 3. Capacity Building.
  • 4. Research and Development.

Inclusion is not a project for us — it’s a principle that guides every program, every outreach, and every voice we amplify.

What We Do

At RKB Foundation, we drive change through impactful programs and partnerships focused on equity, education, and inclusive development.

Education Access

We ensure learners with disabilities have access to quality education through inclusive tools, resources, and outreach.

Policy Advocacy

We influence policy change by working with stakeholders to build frameworks that uphold disability rights and equity.

Capacity Building

We train educators, organizations, and community leaders to deliver more inclusive, accessible, and sustainable programs.

Research & Development

We support research that drives innovative practices, data-driven policy, and accessible education strategies.

Community Engagement

We work at grassroots level to raise awareness, build empathy, and mobilize support for disability inclusion.
