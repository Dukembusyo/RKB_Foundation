Who We Are

RKB Foundation is a non profit organization empowering Persons with Disabilities through Capacity building, Education, Policy Advocacy and Research Consultancy.

Vision Statement:

A society where persons with disabilities are empowered, skilled, and actively shaping an innovative and optimistic future. The Future is Bright

Values:

  • Accountability
  • Leadership
  • Teamwork
  • Inclusivity

Our Mission

To empower Persons with disabilities through capacity building, policy advocacy, education access and research development in order to create an innovative and optimistic community. Our core Objectives:

  • 1. Educational Access:
  • 2. Policy Advocacy.
  • 3. Capacity Building.
  • 4. Research and Development.

Inclusion is not a project for us — it’s a principle that guides every program, every outreach, and every voice we amplify.

CYNTHIA WANDIRA – Director

Lawyer | Advocate | Disability Rights Specialist

Wandira is a seasoned Lawyer and the Director/CEO of RKB Foundation with a strong specialization in capacity building, research, and advocacy for persons with disabilities. She has dedicated her career to promoting inclusion, equity, and empowerment within marginalized communities.

Driven by a deep commitment to social justice, Wandira has led impactful initiatives that strengthen institutional frameworks, influence policy, and enhance access to rights for persons with disabilities. She has worked closely with national and international stakeholders to design and implement inclusive programs, conduct evidence-based research, and champion reforms that bridge legal gaps affecting persons with disabilities.

Under her leadership, RKB Foundation has emerged as a key voice in the disability rights space, advancing systemic change through legal expertise, community engagement, and strategic advocacy.
